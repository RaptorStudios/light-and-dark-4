Privacy

By logging in with Google or Facebook, the user email address will be read, encoded with Bcrypt, and stored in hash format.

  • Email address is only used for generating the hash string necessary for identifying the user's gave saves, and game data.
  • The plaintext email address will not be stored, wrote on logs, transmitted to any third parties, or used for sending mails
  • Aside from the email address, no other personal data will be read
